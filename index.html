Google Sheets Connection Panel

How-To Setup Sync Node:
  1. Open a new Google Sheet in your Google Drive.
  2. Rename three tabs: Applications, Habits, and Todos.
  3. In sheets menu, select Extensions > Apps Script.
  4. Paste the Web App source code. Click the Copy button below to load it to clipboard.
  5. Click Deploy > New Deployment, choose Type: Web App, change Access: Anyone.
  6. Copy the generated Web App URL and paste it right below!
